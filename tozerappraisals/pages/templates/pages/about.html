Who We Are

David Tozer is the owner of Tozer Appraisal Services Ltd. David is a member in good standing with the Appraisal Institute of Canada and has a CRA designation (Certified Residential Appraiser). Staff includes an appraiser candidate and two administrative personnel. We are insured to appraise residential lots, cottage properties, single family residences, and income properties (to a maximum of 4 units).

History

Our appraisal firm was founded by John Tozer in 1972. His son David acquired a CRA (Certified Residential Appraiser) designation from the Appraisal Institute of Canada in 1993 and assumed ownership in 2003. The company has grown steadily over the years. Heavy investment in technology and an internet presence has resulted in superior productivity and industry visibility. We pride ourselves in treating both existing and prospective Clients in a professional and courteous manner. We cordially invite you to contact us to discuss your appraisal needs.
