What We Offer

Sound decisions are best made with quality information. Real estate is often the largest asset the average Canadian owns. As time goes on there are often decisions that must be made with respect to property ownership. Sell or rent? What kind of financing can be achieved? Does a particular property fit in a portfolio? What are the tax consequences of selling a property? Our appraisals can help answer these and other important real estate questions. Our strength lies in our ability to provide you with unbiased and informative reports. We service the general Peterborough area and have membership in three local real estate boards as well as the Appraisal Institute of Canada. We have produced literally thousands of reports over the years and as a result are well acquainted with our coverage area. Our main clientele are the major financial institutions, a number of relocation companies, mortgage brokers, lawyers, and property owners.

We provide services in such areas as:

  • Residential property reports (single family up to 4 unit dwellings)
  • New constructions or "to be renovated"
  • Land Appraisals
  • Estate, Divorce, Relocation, Property Assessment Appeals, etc
  • Consulting
