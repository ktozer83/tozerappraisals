Welcome!

We are a full service residential real estate appraisal company that publishes appraisals primarily for purposes of financing, relocation, marital and estate settlements, asset management, and purchase/sale agreements.

Our company began operating in 1972. We service the general Peterborough area and have membership in three local real estate boards as well as the Appraisal Institute of Canada. We have produced literally thousands of reports over the years and as a result are well acquainted with our coverage area. Our strength lies in our ability to provide you with unbiased and informed valuations. Our main clientele are the major financial institutions, a number of relocation companies, mortgage brokers, and lawyers. Our coverage area extends from Peterborough southerly to Lake Ontario, east to Marmora, north to the Apsley area, and west to the Lindsay area.
